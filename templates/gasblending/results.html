Instructions for Gas Blending

If you would like us to mix the gas for you, please follow these steps:

  1. Ensure you have a suitable gas blending station and equipment.
  2. Check the current oxygen, nitrogen, and helium percentages in the tank.
  3. If necessary, empty the tank to remove the existing gas mixture.
  4. Add the appropriate amounts of oxygen, nitrogen, and helium to the tank using precise gas blending techniques.
  5. Thoroughly mix the gases in the tank to ensure uniform distribution.
  6. Perform gas analysis to verify the final gas mixture meets the desired specifications.
  7. If the gas mixture is within the acceptable range, label the tank with the gas type and percentages.
  8. Store the tank in a secure and well-ventilated area away from heat or ignition sources.
